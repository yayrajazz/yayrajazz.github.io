About us

vision

We believe in a new and changed world, our medium is music. Grounded and standing firm in the Gospel message we deliver spiritual music to uplift you. Because a changed world starts with a changed heart. Every soul should feel the fruits of the spirit at work in his or her life - love- joy-peace-patiece-kindness-goodness-faithfulness-gentleness- self control. 


The band sponsers the project C.D.A.C.R. wich is a new centrum for development through artistic and creative research based in Ghana. Their first project is building a school complex for Kwajwifo DA School in the Krachi Nchumuru district of the Volta region of Ghana. The project is an initiative by band founder Eyram.

Check www.cdacr.ml for more info. 

We support with the half of all the income we make in performances and gigs.
